About PHS

PHS excels in providing client-focused and cutting edge professional development that can be customized to the unique needs of your organization. PHS took the challenge to provide its expertise by giving customized solutions to the clients in various fields such as Catering, Housekeeping Property Management etc. PHS was established in Y2K the New Millennium with the thought to revolutionarise the Unorganized sector by making the entry of Hospitality Professional entrepreneurs. Since the professionals were restricted to Star Hotels in the Hospitality sector, there was a niche segment waiting to get explored comprising of Schools, Business colleges, MNC, Corporate and Hospitals.
